The Yokudans, fleeing the sinking of Yokuda in 1E 792 in the Ra Gada fleet, settled the island of Herne and invaded Hammerfell. These Ra Gada would go on to be called the Redguards and began their conquest infull in 1E 808 in a series of pivitol battles against the Orcs and Bretons of the land. Volenfell was renamed Hammerfell in a rough translation.
