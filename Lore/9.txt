Hammerfell. A tropical land in Western Tamriel that is home to jungles, deserts, grasslands, and more. Bordered by High Rock, Skyrim to the north, and Cyrodiil to the east. Home to the Alik’r Desert, Southern Bangkorai, Craglorn, Khefrem, and Sunforge with many isles around it including Stros M’kai, and The Chain.
